Easy Paper Package

Documents that last.

A plain UTF-8 format for printable documents — no binaries, no internet, no complexity.

Open Editor Read the Docs
Pure UTF-8
Every .epp file is plain text. Open it in Notepad and it reads fine.
No binaries
Zero embedded data. Images, fonts, and rendering are the viewer's job — not the file's.
Offline
The viewer makes no network requests. Your documents never leave your machine unless you choose.
A4 ready
Every page maps to A4 paper. Hit Print and it comes out right.
Easy to parse
Line-oriented, command-based. A working parser fits in under 100 lines of JavaScript.
Human readable
Future-proof by design. In 30 years you can still open an .epp file and read it.
